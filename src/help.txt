Commands:
  add <title> [description]
  - Adds a new item to your to-do list.
  
  remove <pos>
  - Removes item at `pos`, where `pos` is the index in the list starting at 1.
  
  list
  - Lists everything in your to-do list
  
  load <file>
  - Loads a previously saved list from `file`
  
  save <file>
  - Saves your current session to `file`

  help
  - Displays this message
  
* <>: Required, []: Optional
